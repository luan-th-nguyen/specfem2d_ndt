1 # SPECFEM2D
2 
3 SPECFEM2D allows users to perform 2D and 2.5D (i.e., axisymmetric) simulations
4 of acoustic, elastic, viscoelastic, and poroelastic seismic wave propagation.
5 The package can also be used for full waveform imaging (FWI) or adjoint tomography.
6 
7 
8 Main "historical" developers: Dimitri Komatitsch and Jeroen Tromp
9  (there are currently many more!)
